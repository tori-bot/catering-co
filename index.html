Chicken Biryani

Chicken Biryani is a delicious savory rice dish that is loaded with spicy marinated chicken, caramelized onions, and flavorful saffron rice.

Ugali

The infamous ugali is a staple diet of the indigenous people of East Africa. We serve it with a vegetable and/or meat stew.

Nyama Choma

Barbecued meat, usually goat or beef, served roasted with beer and/or side dishes such as ugali.

Matoke

Coconut milk peanut sauce matoke. There are other fun variations too, delivered just how you like it.
